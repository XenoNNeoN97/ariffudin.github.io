UI DESIGN / UX DESIGN
My Role
Sole UI/UX Designer
Duration
4 weeks
Tools
Figma, Photoshop, Illustrator, Lucidchart, Paper & Pencil
Overview
Fitness App, a fitness app, was created as part of a Google UX design course. The app's goal is to "ignite a love for exercising" by supporting users in establishing a solid foundation with basic training videos and instructions.
Design Process
With an emphasis on UI for this project, I covered the following phases in my design process: user stories, proto personas, user flows, sketching, wireframing, style guides, and prototyping.
Define
I started the first step in my process by defining and answering the 5 W questions.
Empathize
Following the definition of the app's scope, the following stage was to create user stories and a proto persona to better understand what consumers' requirements and objectives.
Ideate
I then determined which features the app would require in order to be successful and useful. I also made a user flow diagram to map out these features and determine what type of pages the app would require.
Design
I then began sketching low-fidelity wireframes on paper using a pencil. I then went on to Figma for my mid-fidelity wireframes, where I designed the initial mobile platform layouts.
Style Guide
I made a style guide to show and instruct others on the final choices for all visual aspects, including colors, icons, and typefaces.
Prototype
I continued to use Figma to create my high-fidelity wireframes and an interactive prototype.
Mock Ups
Next Steps
I'd want to test the design by few users to validate the concept so far. It is critical to determine whether the app's flows and visual design are appealing to consumers. I'd also like to identify where there are opportunities for improvement, as iterations are an essential part of the design process.
