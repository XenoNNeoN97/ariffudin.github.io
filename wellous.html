Overview
Wellous is an Asian health food and nutrition company that aims to improve consumer health and wellness through the development, manufacturing, marketing, and selling of its products. With a focus on agile, science-based, and market-driven product development methodology and vertically integrated manufacturing capabilities, the company is able to rapidly launch products and ensure quality ingredients. Wellous distributes its products through tech-enabled channels and employs a social commerce business model by leveraging independent dealers with social media skills and nutrition and fitness knowledge transfer capabilities. The company is headquartered in Kuala Lumpur and operates globally.
what was the problem we were trying to solve?
Wellous aims to assist businesses in improving their health and wellness offerings and staying competitive by providing innovative, flexible, and adaptable solutions. They prioritize creating value and going above and beyond to help their clients achieve their objectives. By doing so, they aim to become the preferred health and wellness solutions provider and distinguish themselves from their competitors. How can we use digital solutions to develop a new and captivating user experience for Wellous customers?
what were my responsibilities?
1. Worked alongside the client to ensure their business objectives were in sync with user goals. 2. Conducted extensive research and carefully examined the findings to steer the design process. 3. Generated a range of low- and high-fidelity designs such as user flows, wireframes, UI graphics, and UI specifications, to bring entire websites to life. 4. Collaborated with project managers and engineers during QA sessions to fine-tune the end user experience and ensure its flawlessness.
The Solution
I have created an online platform to provide personalized health and wellness recommendations based on customers' needs and preferences. The platform can incorporate digital tools, such as wearable devices and mobile apps ( in future ), to collect and analyze data on customers' health behaviors and habits. Customized nutrition and fitness plans, supplement recommendations, and other health and wellness resources can be provided based on the data collected. Interactive features, such as virtual coaching and community forums, can be added to engage customers and gamification elements, such as challenges and rewards, can be added to motivate customers to achieve their health and wellness goals. To ensure a seamless user experience.
